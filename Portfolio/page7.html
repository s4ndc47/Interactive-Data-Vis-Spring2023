Font, Color Scheme

I have opted for the classic fonts Roboto for the body text and Merriweather for the headings, primarily because of their accessibility and cost-effectiveness as free font options.

Aiming to create a stylish and visually appealing website with a modern, media-like aesthetic, I have chosen unusual colors. Additionally, I have incorporated illustrations into graphics to enhance the visual impact, I have employed a black and white treatment for certain photographs. Finally, I have included black and white photographs as illustrations, accompanied by accents of light yellow-green and rosy red color.
