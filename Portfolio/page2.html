About the Data Source

The data used in this project will be obtained from the U.S Census data. The dataset includes population data from 2011 to 2021, excluding 2020 since there is no offical record in this year. including the total population, sex and age, relationship, household by type, educational attainment, and health insurance coverage.

Data Source: U.S. Census Bureau, (2011-2021 except 2010).
